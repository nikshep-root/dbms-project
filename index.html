How FoodBridge Works

🍲

Restaurant Lists Food

Restaurants post surplus food with quantity, expiry time, and category. Takes less than 30 seconds.

🤝

NGO Requests Food

Verified NGOs browse available food, filter by location and type, and request what they need.

🚚

Food Gets Delivered

Admin approves, delivery is tracked in real-time. Food reaches people who need it most.
